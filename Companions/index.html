The Companions of Whiterun

The Companions are warriors of Skyrim. The guild functions similarly to the fighters guilds in the other regions of Tamriel. There has been no official leader of the Companions since the death of Ysgramor.

Currently, members of the Companions are widely known for their triumphs in battle and assistance to the people of Skyrim. Their services may be requested or hired from anyone throughout Skyrim.
