Frequently Asked Questions

Questions about training:


In most cases, yes, you will need to come to Whiterun in order to recieve training. In some instances though, your requested trainer may be in your area for hired services, in which case they may be able to arrange trining at a location more convenient for you.
A typical training session is between 3-5 hours long.


Questions about hiring services:


The Companions provide a wide range of services. From retrieving stolen goods to clearing out skeever infested homes, they are able to do almost any task you require However, they will not harm innocent people. Bandits, assassins, mercenaries, necromancers, wild animals: yes. Annoying neighbors, mischevous children, pesky dogs, strange townsfolk: no.
Even if you aren't local to Whiterun, the Companions travel all across Skyrim.
The cost of services will be determined after requesting and speaking with a Companion. Cost is determined based on location, how extensive the job is, and materials required. Most jobs average 500 gold.
Time to complete a job may vary, but most are done within three days of arrival.
You are allowed to request a specific Companion, but if they are unavailable, a different member will be assigned to your service.
In the rare event that your requests cannot be completed, you will not be charged for service and another Companion will assist you with your requested services.


Questions about joining:

The Companions will accept those from all races and creeds, none are discriminated against.
You do not need any experience to join, but it is recommended to have some combat or magic skill. If chosen, you will recieve all the training and experience necessary to become a legendary Companion of Skyrim.
All those wishing to join will be given a series of tasks to complete, if done successfully, they will be highly considered for a spot among the Companions. These tasks may be completed in your own time and will range from Whiterun to all across Skyrim. Most people accomplish this in about two weeks after arriving to Jorrvaskr.
If accepted, you are highly encouraged to come and live with your fellow Companions in Jorrvaskr or in the town itself. However, a select few wishing to stay in their own city may do so if they are willing to make routine trips to Whiterun for training. Those who do not relocate would recieve their jobs by way of courier.
