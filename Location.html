Getting to Skyrim

Skyrim is the northernmost province of Tamriel, bordering the Sea of Ghosts. If you are travelling from outside of Skyrim, be prepared for cold weather and mountainous terrain.



From High Rock:

To get to Skyrim, travel east till the Drudach Mountains. Markarth will be the first major city that you will reach.

From Hammerfell and the Summerset Isles:

To get to Skyrim, travel northeast until reaching the Drudach Mountains. Markarth or Falkreath will be the first major cities that you reach.

From Cyrodiil, Valenwood, and Elsweyr:

To get to Skyrim, travel due north till reaching the northern border of Cyrodiil or the Pale Pass through Jerall Mountains. Falkreath or Riften will be the first major cities that you reach.

From Morrowind and Black Marsh:

To get to Skyrim, travel northwest until reaching the Velothi Mountains. Riften or Windhelm will be the first major cities that you reach.



Getting to Whiterun

Whiterun is centrally located in Skyrim, making it province's major commercial hub. It is northwest of the Throat of the World, the highest mountain in all of Tamriel.



From Markarth, Solitude, Windhelm, and Riften:

To get to Whiterun, travel to the stables outside of the city and find the carriage. They will take you to Whiterun for a small fee.

From the Hjaalmarch hold:

To get to Whiterun, travel south on the main road. At the fork, turn left, the road sign will point the way.

From Dawnstar:

To get to Whiterun, travel south out of the city. Follow the road, staying straight at the first fork and branching left at the second fork. Right before crossing the White River, take a right at the fork and follow the signs to the Whiterun Gate.

From Winterhold:

To get to Whiterun, travel south on the main road, staying straight at the first and second forks in the road, by Windhelm. At the third fork, in the pale hold, turn left. Right before crossing the White River, take a right at the fork and follow the signs to the Whiterun Gate.

From Falkreath:

To get to Whiterun, take the main road going northwest out of the city, this will bypass Helgen. At the first fork, turn right and stay straight until reaching Riverwood. From there, continue on the road north out of town. Once reaching the fork after the waterfall on the White River, turn left and follow the signs to the Whiterun Gate.



Getting to Jorrvaskr

Jorrvaskr is the oldest building in the city, having been founded before Whiterun even existed. It is located in the Wind District, next to the Skyforge and off of the central plaza.



From the city gate:

Upon entering Whiterun, go straight, through the plains District. Once you reach the small outdoor market area, head left up the stairs, into the Wind District. To the right of the large Gildergreen tree, you will see a smaller set of stairs leading up to Jorrvaskr. Head inside or around back to meet some of the Companions.
